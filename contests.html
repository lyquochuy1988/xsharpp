Instagram Video Story-Telling Competition!

Time: July 1 2022 to September 30 2022

Attention all readers! English Library is hosting a competition on Instagram to test your English skills and creativity! The rules are listed below:


For your story:

• Record yourself reciting an original story you have made!

• Story must be suitable for early readers / readers who are beginners in English.

• All accents are welcomed, because English is global language.

• Recording should be less than 10 minutes.

• The story topic is should be kid and family friendly and sensitive

For submission:

• Follow us on Instagram @english.read.mom

• Post your video on your Instagram account and tag us @english.read.mom

• The posts with the most likes wins!

Prizes:

• First place: $200 Amazon gift card

• Second place: $100 Amazon gift card

• Third place: $50 Amazon gift card


Winners will be determined in the month of October 2022 and notified through Instagram. Contest is open to everyone worldwide but void where prohibited by US or local laws. Employees or associates of the IXI company (IXI Design LLC, Washington) are not allowed to participate.
